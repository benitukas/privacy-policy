# Privacy Policy

**App:** Rąsto Tūris
**Package:** `lt.rastoturis`
**Last updated:** 2026-04-21

## Summary

Rąsto Tūris is a log volume calculator designed for offline use in the field. It does **not** collect personal information, does **not** show ads, does **not** use analytics or tracking, and does **not** send your data to any server we operate. Every measurement you enter stays on your device.

## 1. Data We Collect

**None.** We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information about you.

Specifically, the app does **not** collect:

- Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any contact information
- Location data
- Device identifiers, advertising IDs, or usage analytics
- Crash reports or diagnostic telemetry
- Photos, contacts, files, or any other on-device content outside the app's own storage

## 2. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The app stores the following information **only on your device**, inside Android's private application storage (SharedPreferences):

- Measurements you enter (log length, diameter, quantity)
- Saved calculation sessions and the names you assign them
- Your preferences (language, theme, diameter range, decimal precision, volume threshold)
- A cached flag indicating whether you currently have an active premium subscription

This data never leaves your device. It is not uploaded, shared, or transmitted to us or any third party. If you uninstall the app, Android removes all of this data automatically.

When you export a calculation to an Excel file, the `.xlsx` is written to your device and shared only through Android's system share sheet — **you** choose where it goes (email, messaging app, cloud drive, etc.). We do not receive a copy.

## 3. Third-Party Services

The app uses exactly one third-party service:

### Google Play Billing

To process premium subscription purchases, the app uses Google Play Billing, provided by Google LLC. When you subscribe, the transaction is handled entirely by Google — we never see your payment details, card number, or billing address.

The app communicates with Google Play to verify whether you currently have an active subscription. This communication is between your device and Google; we are not a party to it and receive none of this information.

Google's handling of this data is governed by Google's own privacy policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

## 4. Permissions the App Requests

The app requests only:

- **Internet access** — required exclusively by Google Play Billing to communicate with Google's servers. It is **not** used for any other network activity.
- **Billing** (`com.android.vending.BILLING`) — required to process in-app subscriptions.

The app does not request access to your location, camera, microphone, contacts, SMS, external storage, or any other sensitive permission.

## 5. Security

Because no data is collected or transmitted to us, there is no server-side component that could be breached and no account that could be compromised. Your measurements stay inside the app's private sandbox, which Android isolates from other apps on your device.

The best way to protect the data stored by the app is to protect your device itself: use a screen lock, keep Android up to date, and install apps only from trusted sources.

## 6. Children's Privacy

Rąsto Tūris is a professional tool intended for use in forestry and timber measurement. It is not directed at children under 13 years of age (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction).

We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone, children included. If you believe a child has somehow provided information through this app, please contact us using the address below.

## 7. Your Rights and Choices

Because we do not collect your data, there is nothing for us to show, modify, export, or delete on your behalf. You have full control over the data stored in the app:

- View and edit your measurements and saved sessions inside the app at any time
- Clear all app data by going to **Settings → Apps → Rąsto Tūris → Storage → Clear storage**
- Uninstall the app to delete all local data

For data associated with your subscription (billing records, purchase history), contact Google Play support.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this document and publish the new version at the same URL referenced in the Google Play listing. Material changes will be summarised in the Play Store release notes.
